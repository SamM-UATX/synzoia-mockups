— Section 04 · Setup

Three steps to a life
in the open.

01
Pick a username
Type one in. We hand you a token — four blocks of uppercase letters. Your key to the whole platform. Don't lose it.
02
Install the Shortcut
One tap on iPhone. Paste your token. The Shortcut reads Apple Health — steps, sleep, heart rate, calories — and posts it automatically.
03
Live publicly
Walk. Sleep. Eat. Your numbers show up in real time. Comment. DM. React. Watch each other grow — no algorithm, no feed manipulation.
